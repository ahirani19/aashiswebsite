art & design

i love to draw and dabble in the arts in my free time. here are some of my favorite pieces of artwork!

lesion

a piece made entirely in colored pencil. this piece was made to represent the feeling of being boxed in, and what it feels like to hide parts of yourself, especially when the front begins to shatter. i inlcuded two detail shots: the first is of the hand, which i spent time on to really show the stress and grip, as well as light reflection, and the second is of the sparks and brightness of the fireworks crackling. i really wanted the fireworks to be somewhat focal, as the brightness should almost distrat you from the stress and agony of the subject.
